Starter prompts

Explain a variance

I work in Finance at KGM Australia. Review the table below and write a plain-English variance summary.
Include: biggest movements, likely questions management may ask, and items requiring verification.
Do not invent reasons. If the data does not show the cause, say so.

Draft AP/AR email

Draft a polite Australian English email about [invoice/payment/discrepancy].
Audience: [supplier/dealer/internal team]
Goal: [request correction / ask for payment status / explain discrepancy]
Tone: professional, clear and calm.
Keep it under 150 words.
