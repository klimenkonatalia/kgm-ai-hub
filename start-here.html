How to get useful answers from AI.

Good AI output is mostly about context. Tell it the task, audience, source material, constraints and output format.

Prompt formulaContextReviewExample

The simple formula

Use this structure when you want a reliable result:

Role: You are helping KGM Australia.
Task: What you need done.
Context: Background, audience, model, department, source material.
Output: Format, length, tone, table/bullets/email.
Constraints: What to avoid, what must be included.
Review: Ask it to flag assumptions and missing information.

Context matters

AI does not automatically know our business, dealer network, tone, current offers, internal processes or the purpose of your task. Give it enough context so it can help like a capable new starter, not a mind reader.

  • Who is the audience?
  • What decision will this support?
  • What does good output look like?
  • What should it not include?

Always review

AI can be confident and still be wrong. Check facts, numbers, tone, dates, claims, prices, model details and anything customer-facing before using the output.

Do not paste passwords, credentials, security-sensitive information, confidential client data or sensitive company data into AI tools.

Starter prompt

I work at KGM Australia in [department].
I need help with [task].
Audience: [who will read/use this].
Context: [paste non-sensitive background].
Please create [format].
Tone: clear, practical and professional.
Before answering, list any assumptions or missing information.
