Starter prompts

Prepare a dealer meeting

I work in Sales at KGM Australia. Help me prepare for a dealer meeting.
Use the notes below and create:
1. A short dealer performance summary
2. Three questions to ask
3. Three suggested actions
4. A concise follow-up email draft
Tone: practical, professional, Australian English.
Do not invent numbers or claims.

Research competitor offers

Research current Australian competitor offers for utes and SUVs relevant to KGM.
Focus on Toyota, Ford, Isuzu, Mitsubishi, GWM and BYD.
Return a table with offer type, price/message if available, source link, target audience and what this could mean for KGM sales conversations.
Flag anything that must be verified before use.
