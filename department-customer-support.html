Draft customer response

Known facts: [paste facts]
Goal: acknowledge, explain next step, avoid blame, avoid overpromising.
Tone: empathetic, practical and professional.

Summarise complaints

Analyse the customer comments below.
Group them into themes, estimate sentiment, list the most common issues, and suggest what information the support team should prepare.
Do not identify individuals.

Tip
