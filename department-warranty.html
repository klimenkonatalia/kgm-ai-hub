Starter prompts

Summarise warranty case

Summarise this warranty case for internal review.
Include: vehicle, concern, diagnosis, repair, evidence provided, missing information, and questions for the dealer.
Do not decide approval. Flag anything that requires warranty team judgement.

Request missing information

Draft a professional email to a dealer requesting missing warranty claim information.
Include: what is missing, why it is needed, and next step.
Tone: helpful, clear, not accusatory.
