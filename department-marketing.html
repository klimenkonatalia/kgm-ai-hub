Starter prompts

Create campaign ideas

Act as a marketing assistant for KGM Australia.
Create 5 campaign concepts for [model] targeting [audience].
For each concept include: headline, key message, channel, visual idea, and why it fits KGM.
Tone: confident, practical, not exaggerated.
Do not invent pricing or offers.

Generate image brief

Create an image generation brief for a KGM campaign banner.
Model: [model]
Audience: [audience]
Mood: premium, practical, Australian automotive
Format: website hero + LinkedIn image + dealer tile
Include what should be visible, what to avoid, and suggested copy overlay.
