How to use this workflow

Copy one prompt, add your real context, attach a file if needed, then keep the conversation going. The first answer is rarely the final answer. Ask follow-up questions like “make it shorter”, “turn it into a table”, “focus on Australian dealers”, or “what should I verify?”.

Remember: AI cannot read your mind. If you do not explain the audience, purpose, constraints and source material, it will guess.

EOFY offers research

Research current Australian offers for Toyota HiLux, Ford Ranger, Isuzu D-MAX, Mitsubishi Triton, GWM Cannon and KGM Musso. Summarise offer type, price/value message, finance offer, warranty, key disclaimers and positioning. Include links to sources and flag anything that needs human verification.

Customer review analysis

Analyse customer reviews for [model]. Identify common praise, common complaints, likely target audience, buying motivations and possible messaging opportunities for KGM. Separate verified facts from interpretation.

Market trends

Research current Australian ute and SUV market trends. Focus on value, warranty, hybrid/EV interest, fleet buyer needs, towing, safety and cost of ownership. Summarise implications for KGM Australia.
