Starter prompts

Supplier email

Draft a professional supplier email about [parts order / availability / discrepancy].
Include: part numbers, quantities, required response date, and next step.
Tone: direct, polite, Australian English.

Stock summary

Analyse the parts data below.
Identify fast-moving items, slow-moving items, possible stock risks, and actions to consider.
Return a table and a short summary. Do not invent demand drivers.

Tip

Start small. Use AI for a first draft, a summary, a comparison table or a checklist. Then check it against your own expertise before using it.
